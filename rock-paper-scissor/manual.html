How to Play Rock, Paper & Scissors!

The goal of Rock, Paper, Scissors is simple: You and your opponent each choose one of the three hand gestures—Rock, Paper, or Scissors—and compare them to see who wins based on the following rules:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Understand the Symbols

  • Rock

A fist with your thumb pointing up.

  • Paper

An open hand with fingers extended.

  • Scissors

A fist with the index and middle fingers extended, forming a "V."

Step 2: Selecting the gestures—

Click on the any button that you want to select as your gesture. Your Opponent (CPU) will also choose it's sign for the round and wait for the result.

Step 3: Compare the Gestures

Rock vs Scissors

  • Rock crushes Scissors!

    • If you pick Rock and your opponent picks Scissors, Rock wins.

    Scissors vs Paper

  • Scissors cut Paper!

    • If you pick Scissors and your opponent picks Paper, Scissors win.

    Paper vs Rock

  • Paper covers Rock!

    • If you pick Paper and your opponent picks Rock, Paper wins.

    Tie Scenario

  • If both players choose the same gesture, it’s a tie.

    • Example: Both pick Rock, so no one wins. You can replay the round!

    Step 4: Scoring

    If you win you'll awarded with '1' point, but if you lose you're losing score will also increases. You neither get a point nor lose if it is a tie!

    Basic Strategy Tips:

    1. Randomization: Don’t pick the same gesture too often. Try to mix it up to keep your opponent guessing.
    2. Psychological Play: Pay attention to your Opponent (CPU) moves. Try to guess it before hand!

    This is a simple yet fun game that can be played anywhere, anytime. Whether for decision-making or just for fun, Rock, Paper, Scissors brings a quick and playful competition to any moment!
